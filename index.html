FAQ

Questions buyers ask

What is Opportunity Assist?

Opportunity Assist is an opportunity intelligence platform from My True Prosperity that helps mission-driven organizations find, score, track, and pursue funding opportunities — combining discovery, fit scoring, pursuit recommendations, contract renewal tracking, capture pipeline management, grant and RFP support, and AI-powered summaries.

Who is Opportunity Assist built for?

Workforce development organizations, youth employment providers, financial capability agencies, VITA coalitions, CRA practitioners, community-based organizations, employability programs, and small to midsize mission-driven organizations.

Is Opportunity Assist a grant database?

No. A grant database helps you identify funders. Opportunity Assist scores fit, recommends what to pursue, and manages the full capture pipeline across grants, contracts, RFPs, and procurement.

Is Opportunity Assist an AI grant writer?

No. AI grant writers help draft language. Opportunity Assist helps you decide what to pursue and manage the pursuit. Proposal support is part of the broader capture lifecycle, not the whole product.

How is it different from SAM.gov, BidNet, Bonfire, Instrumentl, or Candid?

Bid portals show what is posted. Grant databases help identify funders. AI grant writers help draft language. Traditional GovCon tools support large contractors. Opportunity Assist is built for mission-driven organizations that need a practical way to find, score, track, and pursue the right opportunities.

Can it help decide whether an opportunity is worth pursuing?

Yes. The AI Fit Score evaluates fit, past performance, geography, capacity, partnerships, budget, and risk, then returns a clear recommendation to support bid/no-bid decisions.

Can small nonprofits use Opportunity Assist?

Yes. It is designed specifically for small and midsize mission-driven organizations, not large government contractors.

What does “Stop chasing. Start choosing.” mean?

It means spending less energy reacting to every posting and more on the opportunities that genuinely fit your mission and capacity.
